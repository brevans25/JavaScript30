The Bird Catalog of O. Messiaen

Olivier Messiaen (1942 - 2002) was a French composer, organist and teacher. During his life, Messiaen taught several musicians who became very important composers, including Pierre Boulez, Karlheinz Stockhausen and Almeida Prado.

In addition to being an exceptional teacher and composer, Messiaen was also an ornithologist. His love for the birds led him to compose several pieces inspired in such creatures.

Among these pieces, we can highlight the famous Catalog des Oiseaux written for solo piano, the collection of Oiseaux Exotiques for solo piano and orchestra, and Chronochromie for large orchestra, which is considered the culmination of this bird-inspired period of Messiaen's life.
