P. Glass and The Hours

Philip Glass, an american composer born in 1937, is considered one of the first composers to experiment with what is known nowadays as minimalist music.

In addition to composing traditional classical music, Glass is a prolific composer of film music. His music can be heard in several movies, including North Star, The Truman Show, and my personal favorite: The Hours.
